Featured Project: Chain Reaction
Chain Reaction is a steam-punk puzzle game make in Processing where two players must work together to fix a variety of broken machines.
On top of the main puzzle mode there is also a freeplay mode where players can create their own fun contraption.
The steampunk vector art style we went with was a lot of fun to work with, and produced some nice results.
