Take a walk for 10 - 30 minutes

The external framing for your experience should be around water in your environment. Pause when you encounter water in your walk - this can be an intentional destination, a serendipitous discovery, a reflective insight, or a vague awareness. Consider that all the water that is on the Earth has always been here, and will always be, in one form or another, visiting each of us as we spend a short time here with it. Consider the life of the water as it moves, where it has been before, where it is headed, and the impact of its prescense. Consider the impact as water has drawn upon, modified, and left its imprint on the land: the place it once was, is not currently, but could be again.
